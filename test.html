Project Components

Crop Management

explains crop types and growing mechanisms tailored to geographical differences:

  • highlights regional crop practices.
  • focuses on packaging and parity of crops.
  • promotes sustainable productivity.

Horticulture

focuses on the types and growth processes of horticultural crops:

  • includes fruits, flowers, vegetables, and spices.
  • guides from seed selection to harvesting.
  • supports 200+ languages with google translate api.

Weather Tracking

provides real-time weather updates and alerts:

  • tracks cyclones in real-time.
  • offers hourly weather updates for 7 days.
  • keeps farmers informed of market impacts.

Resource Calculation

includes tools for efficient resource use:

  • resource forecasting: water, time, and fertilizer needs.
  • price prediction and comparison tools.
  • crop recommendations based on location, seasons, and soil rotation.

Markets

features tools for connecting farmers and buyers:

  • marketplace for selling and buying produce.
  • locates nearby cold storages and markets.
  • crisis call support for emergencies.
