Contents :

Crop Management

explains crop types and growing mechanisms tailored to geographical differences:

  1. highlights regional crop practices.
  2. focuses on Nutritional and general properties of crops.
  3. Gives insights on its market value and demand Across the country.

Horticulture

focuses on the types and growth processes of horticultural crops:

  1. includes fruits, flowers, vegetables, spices And cash crops.
  2. Growing mechanism from seed selection to harvesting.
  3. Linguistic support with 200+ languages with google translate api.

Weather Tracking

provides real-time weather updates and alerts:

  1. Helps in tracking cyclones in real-time, Using weather mapping facilities.
  2. offers hourly weather updates for Consecutive 7 days.
  3. keeps farmers Updated with News and Timely alerts.

Resource Calculation

includes tools for efficient resource use:

  1. resource forecasting: water, Seeds, time, fertilizers and area needs.
  2. market price prediction and comparison tools.
  3. crop recommendations based on location, seasons, and soil rotation.

Markets

features tools for connecting farmers and buyers:

  1. marketplace for selling and buying produce Securely across the country.
  2. locates nearby cold storages and markets.
  3. crisis call support for All types of emergencies.
